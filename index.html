Dongwoo KIM

Dongwoo Kim is Lausanne based interaction designer from Korea. He brings imaginative miracles for everyone though his interactive works in mundane life. He was born in Seoul, Republic of Korea and studied Design&Space in ESAAA(L’Ecole Supérieure d’Art Annecy Alpes), France. Now he lives and works based in Lausanne, Switzerland where he is studying BA Média interaction design at ECAL(Ecole cantonale d'art de Lausanne).
