Dongwoo KIM

My name is Dongwoo Kim, I’m visual interaction designer from Republic of Korea. I graduated from ECAL Ecole Cantonale D’Art de Lausanne, in Lausanne Switzerland from Visual Communication with a minor in Media Interaction Design. I’ve created and experienced a variety of tasks including web applications, interactive objects, installations and UI/UX through Design Thinking and various programming languages.  I am currently an Interaction Designer at Giantstep. And also I work at the Interaction Studio Reformation.ooo in Zurich, Switzerland.
